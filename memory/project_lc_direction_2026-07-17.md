## The direction (Andy, 2026-07-17)

After the plans-portfolio review (capture/organization layer complete
through Trips T1–T9; give-back layer nearly absent; ~176 open QA items),
Andy set the direction now canonical in
`docs/plans/2026-07-17-spine-and-share-roadmap.md`:

**Track A — correct skeletal residential spine, birth to now, in WEEKS
not months.** The app must actively guide the user there. Full elaboration
of a life is by design a *years-long* project — surfaces showing remaining
work must be extensive yet **undaunting**, all encouragement and
invitation, never an overdue to-do list. Completion pressure applies to
the spine skeleton only.

**Track B — a shareable, fairly complete deliverable.** Two shareable
elements: the spine itself, and the new **shareable collection** concept —
one or more recollections around a past experience, shared with people who
were part of it (or significant others), who collaborate via commentary
and feedback. This widens Single Post Share (Step 12,
[[project_lc_single_post_share]]) from single post → experience
collection; enrichment-invitation model unchanged; requires a
minimum-viable slice of Access Cards (Step 13).

## The two design-first units

1. **Loose-Ends surface** — Dashboard reincarnation gathering unsequenced
   residences, draft trips, open jots, review proposals, user-asserted
   spine gaps (never date-computed — invariant #5), Future Places. Also
   absorbs Step 8's unspecced orchestrated strand (assistant nudging off
   `chronicle/threshold.reached`) — passive face + active face, one
   design doc.
2. **Shareable Collections** — collection object (synthesis-layer,
   journalist-model narrative per the un-deferred 2026-06-14
   interview-dialogue design) + token share + minimal privacy gate + the
   2026-07-09 external-links/media question folded in.

## QA principle

All open QA consolidated into ONE sequence,
`docs/qa/2026-07-17-master-qa-sequence.md`, prioritized to the objectives
(spine correctness → loose-ends machinery → context/collection substrate →
trips → polish), NOT build order. Each phase = one sitting; findings get a
same-week remediation pass; the pin-facts editor rides the first
globe-region remediation.
